Privacy Policy

I keep this simple — the same way I'd explain it on a job site.

Who I am. This site is The SEO Harvest — Adam Abshire, a one-person local SEO service in Fort Worth, Texas.

What I collect. If you fill out the "Where You Rank" form, I collect what you type in: your name, business name, trade, your website or Facebook page, and your email. That's it — nothing else, and nothing financial.

Why I collect it. I use it to put together your free visibility report, email it to you, and send the occasional helpful tip. Nothing else. Tell me to stop any time and I will.

Who I share it with. I don't sell your information and I don't hand it to advertisers. Your form details sit in my own private records so I can prepare your report.

Analytics. Like most sites, this one uses a tool (Microsoft Clarity) that shows me how visitors use the pages — what they read, where they click — so I can make the site clearer. That can include anonymized recordings of page activity and heatmaps. It's about how pages are used, not about identifying you personally.

Cookies. That tool may set cookies in your browser. You can block or clear cookies in your browser settings any time.

Your choices. Want to see what I have, change it, or have me delete it? Reach out through the form on the site, or reply to any email from me, and I'll take care of it.

Changes. If any of this changes, I'll update this page.
